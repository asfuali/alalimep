Who We Are

Al Ali MEP Contracting is a premier industrial service provider that has established a strong presence within the industrial corridors of West Bengal and Odisha. Founded in Kharagpur, we began our journey by tackling complex challenges in mechanical maintenance and structural fabrication. Over the years, we have evolved into a comprehensive MEP contracting firm, offering a wide range of services that include mechanical, electrical, and plumbing solutions tailored for heavy industries such as steel manufacturing, power generation, and petrochemicals.

We are a strategic technical partner with the steel industry leaders and other leading industrial players. Our transition towards becoming a Pvt. Ltd. entity reflects our professional evolution.
